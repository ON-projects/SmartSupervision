Gymnasium

Our gymnasium is equipped with modern fitness equipment and facilities for a variety of sports. The gym provides a space for physical education classes and team practices.

Our comprehensive physical education curriculum is designed to teach students the fundamentals of various sports, promote physical fitness, and encourage healthy lifestyles.

For students who wish to compete at a higher level, we have competitive sports teams that participate in regional and national tournaments.

Our gymnasium is designed to be inclusive, ensuring that all students, regardless of their physical abilities, can participate in and benefit from physical activities.
