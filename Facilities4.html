Football field

We have a field for soccer and other outdoor activities. These fields are maintained to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all students.

Our primary outdoor sports field is a versatile space that can be used for various sports, including football and running.

Safety is paramount at our outdoor sports facilities. All activities are supervised by trained coaches and staff members who ensure that students follow safety guidelines and use equipment properly.

In addition to organized sports, the outdoor sports field is available for recreational activities during free periods and after school.
